Startup India is a Government of India initiative that supports startups through funding access, tax benefits, regulatory ease, and ecosystem support. It is managed by DPIIT under the Ministry of Commerce & Industry.

Objectives

  • Simplify regulations
  • Enable funding & market access
  • Promote innovation and job creation
  • Build a strong startup ecosystem

Eligibility

  • Incorporated in India (Pvt Ltd / LLP / Partnership)
  • Within 10 years of incorporation
  • Annual turnover ≤ ₹100 crore
  • Innovative and scalable business model

Key Benefits

  • 100% tax exemption for 3 years
  • Angel Tax exemption
  • ₹10,000 crore Fund of Funds via SIDBI
  • Self-certification for labour & environmental laws
  • 80% rebate on patent filing
  • Access to government tenders & GeM

Registration Steps

  1. 1 Visit startupindia.gov.in
  2. 2 Create an account
  3. 3 Apply for DPIIT Recognition
  4. 4 Receive Startup Certificate

Support Programs

  • Startup India Seed Fund Scheme
  • Credit Guarantee Scheme
  • Incubator Support
  • Mentorship Programs
